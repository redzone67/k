“if you look at where inflation expectations are, our credibility is right where it needs to be.”

“And I know it’s very common now to say the problem is the president’s being so mean to these guys.” 		政治から離れろ。議会に説明責任を果たせ。独立性（民主的正統性）は努力で獲得するものだ
